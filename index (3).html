About Pomodoro ▼

Welcome to Pomodoro - Your Intelligent Study Companion!

Pomodoro leverages the proven Pomodoro Technique, a time management method particularly for STEM subjects that uses 25-minute study sessions followed by short breaks to optimize learning and prevent mental fatigue.

Unlike traditional calendars that simply track exam dates, Pomodoro intelligently calculates your required study sessions based on your course content and timeline. This adaptive approach ensures you're adequately prepared, even when unexpected events like snow days disrupt your schedule.

By breaking down your study material into manageable sessions and tracking your progress, a feature specifically implemented for the neurodiverse community, Pomodoro helps you maintain a consistent and effective study routine while avoiding last-minute cramming.

How It Works ▼

Getting Started with Pomodoro:

  1. Enter your exam details:
    • Subject name (e.g., "Biology Chapter 5")
    • Number of subtopics in the unit
    • Exam date
  2. Pomodoro will automatically calculate your study schedule by:
    • Determining the days available until your exam
    • Allocating 30-minute study sessions per subtopic
    • Distributing these sessions evenly across your available days
  3. Click "Study" to begin a focused 25-minute session, followed by a 5-minute break
  4. For the neurodiverse community or anyone to track the statistics, a monthly progress and upcoming exams feature.
