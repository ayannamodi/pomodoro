About the App: Pomodoro leverages the proven Pomodoro Technique, a time management method particularly for STEM subjects that uses 25-minute study sessions followed by short breaks to optimize learning and prevent
mental fatigue. Unlike traditional calendars that simply track exam dates, Pomodoro intelligently calculates your required study sessions based on your course content and timeline. This adaptive approach ensures
you're adequately prepared, even when unexpected events like snow days disrupt your schedule. By breaking down your study material into manageable sessions and tracking your progress, a feature specifically
implemented for the neurodiverse community, Pomodoro helps you maintain a consistent and effective study routine while avoiding last-minute cramming
